# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

BrowserUse is a small open-source proof of concept, and we want collaboration around it to stay useful, respectful, and grounded in real technical discussion.

Please be constructive, direct, and kind. Disagreement is fine. Harassment, personal attacks, intimidation, discrimination, or bad-faith disruption are not.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in issues, pull requests, and reviews.
- Keep criticism focused on the product, code, docs, or decisions.
- Share reproducible details when reporting problems.
- Help keep the repo understandable for first-time contributors.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or abusive language
- Personal attacks
- Hate speech or discriminatory behavior
- Spam or intentional disruption
- Posting private data, credentials, or sensitive screenshots

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates this code of conduct and may limit participation when needed to keep the project healthy.
